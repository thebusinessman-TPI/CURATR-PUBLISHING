'''
# CURATR Manual Posting Workflow

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to the CURATR manual posting workflow! This guide provides a step-by-step process for publishing our 217 ready-to-post collections to Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts. This workflow is designed to be efficient, user-friendly, and to ensure consistency across all our social media channels.

**Your primary tool will be the `manual_posting_tool.html`, which you can find in the `tools` directory of the `CURATR-PUBLISHING` repository.**

## 2. Using the Manual Posting Tool

The manual posting tool is the central hub for our posting workflow. It provides all the content you need for each post, including the video, hook, caption, and hashtags.

### 2.1. Opening the Tool

1.  Navigate to the `CURATR-PUBLISHING/tools` directory on your local machine.
2.  Open the `manual_posting_tool.html` file in your web browser (Google Chrome is recommended).

### 2.2. Navigating the Tool

-   **Platform Tabs:** At the top of the page, you will see tabs for **Instagram**, **TikTok**, and **YouTube**. Click on the platform you are posting to.
-   **Filters:** You can filter the collections by **Brand** and **Posting Status** (Pending or Posted).
-   **Search:** Use the search bar to find a specific collection by its ID or keywords in the content.
-   **Collection Cards:** Each card represents a collection and contains the following:
    -   **Video Preview:** A preview of the video for the post.
    -   **Content:** The hook, caption, and hashtags for the selected platform.
    -   **Actions:** Buttons to **Copy All Content**, **Download Video**, and **Mark as Posted**.

## 3. Step-by-Step Posting Process

Follow these steps for each collection you post.

### Step 1: Select a Collection

1.  Open the `manual_posting_tool.html`.
2.  Choose the platform you are posting to.
3.  Use the filters to find a collection that is **Pending**.

### Step 2: Prepare Your Content

1.  Click the **"Download Video"** button to save the video to your computer or phone.
2.  Click the **"Copy All Content"** button to copy the hook, caption, and hashtags to your clipboard.

### Step 3: Post to Social Media

#### Instagram (Reel)

1.  Open the Instagram app and create a new Reel.
2.  Upload the video you downloaded.
3.  Paste the copied content into the caption field.
4.  **Important:** Move the hook to the beginning of the caption and the hashtags to the end.
5.  Tag the brand's Instagram account in the post.
6.  Add any relevant trending audio.
7.  Share the Reel.

#### TikTok

1.  Open the TikTok app and create a new video.
2.  Upload the video you downloaded.
3.  Paste the copied content into the caption field.
4.  Add trending audio and any relevant effects.
5.  Share the video.

#### YouTube Shorts

1.  Open the YouTube app and create a new Short.
2.  Upload the video you downloaded.
3.  Paste the copied content into the title and description fields.
4.  Add the hashtags to the description.
5.  Share the Short.

### Step 4: Update the Posting Status

1.  Go back to the `manual_posting_tool.html`.
2.  Find the collection you just posted.
3.  Click the **"Mark as Posted"** button. The card will now be marked as "POSTED".

## 4. Best Practices

-   **Post consistently:** Aim to post a few collections each day to maintain a consistent presence on social media.
-   **Engage with your audience:** Respond to comments and questions on your posts.
-   **Stay up-to-date with trends:** Pay attention to trending audio, formats, and challenges on each platform.
-   **Use high-quality video:** Always use the video provided in the tool to ensure the best quality.

## 5. Questions?

If you have any questions about this workflow, please don't hesitate to ask!
'''
