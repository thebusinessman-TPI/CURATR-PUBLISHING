## üéØ Daily Posting Strategy

### Recommended Schedule
- **Instagram:** 2-3 posts per day (9am, 2pm, 7pm)
- **TikTok:** 3-5 posts per day (spread throughout day)
- **YouTube Shorts:** 1-2 posts per day (morning and evening)

### Content Selection
1. Use **Brand Filter** to focus on specific brands
2. Use **Search** to find specific products or themes
3. Mix different outfit types (single, paired, trifecta, quartet, etc.)
4. Track what performs well and post more of that

### Time Management
- **5 minutes per post** (all 3 platforms)
- **15 minutes total** for 1 collection across all platforms
- **1 hour** = 4 collections = 12 posts total

## üêõ Troubleshooting

### Tool Won't Load
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page (F5)
- Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)

### Video Won't Download
- Try again (sometimes network hiccups)
- If it opens in new tab, right-click and "Save Video As"
- Check Downloads folder - might already be there

### Content Won't Copy
- Try clicking "Copy Content" again
- Manually select and copy from preview
- Check clipboard by pasting in Notepad

### Collections Not Showing
- Check filters (might be filtering everything out)
- Reset filters to "All Brands" and "All Status"
- Clear search box

## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Batch Your Work**
   - Pick 5 collections at once
   - Download all videos first
   - Then post them all in sequence

2. **Use Filters Strategically**
   - Focus on one brand per day
   - Mark as posted immediately after posting
   - Use "Pending" filter to see what's left

3. **Test Different Times**
   - Track which posting times get best engagement
   - Adjust your schedule based on performance
   - Instagram Insights and TikTok Analytics are your friends

4. **Mix Content Types**
   - Don't post all "trifecta" in a row
   - Vary between single, paired, quartet, etc.
   - Different formats keep feed interesting

5. **Engage After Posting**
   - Reply to comments quickly
   - Like and respond to engagement
   - Use first hour after posting to boost algorithm
